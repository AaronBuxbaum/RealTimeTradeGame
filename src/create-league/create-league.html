The bid amount must be at least ${{ctrl.minBid | number: 2}}. Minimum bid is derived from the maximum number of players and the close date. The more the player cap is raised and the longer the event, the more the minimum bid will rise.
Winner will earn at least ${{ ctrl.totalWinnings() | number:2}} The calculation for winner's earnings assume that the maximum number of players allowed will play, all paying in to the given bid number. In reality, this number could be smaller if the league is not filled when it begins.

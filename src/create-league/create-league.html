The bid amount must be at least {{ctrl.minBid | currency}}. Minimum bid is derived from the maximum number of players and the close date.
The more players involved and the longer the event, the more the minimum bid will rise.
Winner will earn {{ ctrl.totalWinnings() | currency}} The calculation for winner's earnings assume that the maximum number of players allowed will play, all paying in to the bid number.
In reality, this number could be smaller if the league is not filled.
